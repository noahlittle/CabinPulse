Quick Setup Guide

Setting Up Your CabinPulse

Get your cabin monitoring up and running in less than 5 minutes with these simple steps.

1

Unpack Your Device

Your CabinPulse package should include:

  • CabinPulse device
  • Power adapter
  • Quick start guide
  • Activation code
2

Power Up

Connect your CabinPulse to power:

  • Plug the power adapter into any standard outlet
  • Connect the adapter to your CabinPulse device
  • Wait for the LED indicator to begin glowing white (about 30 seconds)

Having problems?

Place your device in a central location of your cabin, away from large metal objects that might interfere with the cellular signal.

3

Activate Online

Complete the activation process:

  • Visitdashboard.cabinpulse.com
  • Sign In with the credentials you used at purchase
  • Follow the instructions to activate your device
  • Find the device activation code on the card that came with your device

Can't find your activation code?

Your activation code can be found on the card that came with your device; it's an 8-character code.
