We Build Remote Monitoring Solutions That Actually Work

We're a small Saskatchewan-based team who got tired of unreliable, expensive monitoring solutions that didn't work in Canada. So we built our own.

CabinPulse monitors cabins, vacation homes, RVs, warehouses, and any remote property where reliable connectivity and instant alerts matter most.

Our Story

It started with those dreaded winter drives to check on our properties. Long trips just to see if the pipes had frozen, if the power was out, or if everything was still okay.

We tried security cameras pointed at thermometers, relied on neighbors' spotty WiFi, and paid $140+ monthly for satellite internet just to use smart home devices that wouldn't work during power outages anyway.

As software developers, we knew there had to be a better solution. So we built a prototype with off-the-shelf components for family and friends. The interest exploded.

That's when we invested in creating a certified, reliable product for Canada and the United States – one that we use ourselves and trust completely to keep running for years without human intervention.

CabinPulse is now monitoring cabins, vacation homes, vacant houses, workshops, warehouses, storage facilities, airport buildings, RVs, greenhouses, server rooms, industrial facilities and more - throughout Canada, the United States and Mexico.
