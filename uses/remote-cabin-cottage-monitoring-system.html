Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly does it monitor?

CabinPulse monitors power outages (and restorations), ambient temperature, air quality (VOCs), and relative humidity. You can expand monitoring with CabinPulse Add-Ons, including water leak detection and additional environmental sensors.

Does it need WiFi?

No. CabinPulse uses cellular technology to transmit data. It does not rely on your cabin's internet router, making it immune to internet outages or seasonal disconnects.

Can I cancel my expensive cabin internet?

Yes! Many users cancel expensive year-round internet services (like Starlink) during months they're not visiting their cottage or cabin once they have CabinPulse, as it provides essential monitoring without the high monthly cost.

Can it prevent frozen pipes?

By providing timely low-temperature alerts, CabinPulse gives you the crucial early warning needed to take action (like calling a neighbour or HVAC tech) to prevent pipes from freezing and bursting.
