Your Cabin & Cottage Monitoring Questions Answered

What does CabinPulse monitor for cabins/cottages?

CabinPulse monitors for power outages, ambient temperature, air quality, relative humidity, and listens for standard smoke/CO alarm sound patterns (informational only).

How does it help with power outages at the cabin?

CabinPulse sends you an immediate text/email alert if the power goes out and when it's restored. Its built-in battery keeps it running for up to 3 days, so you always know what's happening, even if you're not there.

Can it prevent frozen pipes in my cottage?

By providing timely low-temperature alerts, CabinPulse gives you the crucial early warning needed to take action and prevent pipes from freezing and bursting, saving you from potentially thousands in damages.

How does air quality monitoring help during wildfire season?

CabinPulse detects VOCs and changes in your IAQ score, which can indicate the presence of wildfire smoke. You'll know if the air at your cabin or cottage is unhealthy before you travel, or if you need to take steps like closing windows or running air purifiers.

How does humidity monitoring protect my cabin?

High humidity can lead to mould, mildew, and structural issues. CabinPulse alerts you if humidity exceeds your set threshold, allowing you to address potential moisture problems before they become serious.

What is smoke alarm sound monitoring?

CabinPulse's acoustic sensor can detect the specific T3 smoke alarm sound patterns. If detected, it sends an informational alert. It's an added layer of awareness, but not a replacement for interconnected or monitored alarm systems.

Do I really not need WiFi? How's the cellular coverage?

Absolutely no WiFi needed! CabinPulse has a built-in cellular modem that connects to all major networks across Canada and the US and works in Mexico too. If there's even a faint signal from any carrier, CabinPulse will work.

Can I save money on my cabin internet bill?

Yes! Many users cancel expensive year-round internet services (like Starlink at $140+/month) at their cabin/cottage once they have CabinPulse, as it provides essential monitoring without needing WiFi, saving significant money, especially in the off-season.
