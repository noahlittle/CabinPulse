Why Traditional Monitors Fail

Most smart home devices rely on WiFi. When the power goes out, your router dies, and your "smart" monitor goes silent right when you need it most.

WiFi Goes Down

Standard monitors require internet. No power means no internet, leaving you blind to what's happening at your property.

Pipes Freeze Fast

Without heat, a property can drop below freezing in 24-48 hours, leading to catastrophic pipe bursts.

Costly Repairs

The average insurance claim for water damage from frozen pipes exceeds $15,000. Prevention is cheaper.

01. RELIABILITY

Cellular, Not WiFi.

CabinPulse has its own dedicated cellular connection (like a smartphone). It doesn't care if your property's internet is down or if the router is unplugged. It just works.

  • Connects to strongest signal automatically
  • No WiFi setup or passwords required
  • Works in remote rural locations
02. ALWAYS ON

Built-in Battery Backup

When the grid goes dark, CabinPulse stays awake. Our internal battery keeps the device monitoring and sending alerts for up to 3 days after power loss.

  • Instant switch to battery power
  • Sends "Power Restored" alerts when grid returns
  • Recharges automatically
03. COMPLETE PICTURE

More Than Just Power

A power outage usually means no heat. CabinPulse monitors temperature and humidity simultaneously, so you know if your property is approaching freezing conditions.

  • Set custom temperature thresholds
  • Monitor humidity levels to prevent mold
  • Historical data charts in the dashboard

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a SIM card or data plan?

No. CabinPulse comes pre-configured with a multi-network SIM card built-in. The connectivity is handled entirely by us through your simple subscription plan. It works right out of the box.

Does it work if the power outage is very short?

Yes, CabinPulse detects outages immediately. However, to prevent spamming your phone for 1-second flickers, you can set a "delay threshold" in the dashboard (e.g., only alert me if power is out for more than 5 minutes).

How many people can receive alerts?

You can add unlimited contacts for email alerts and multiple contacts for SMS text alerts. You can ensure your property manager, spouse, or neighbor gets the message.

Does it work on a 12V or 24V system?

Yes, CabinPulse is compatible with both 12V and 24V systems with the appropriate adapter. You can easily connect it for monitoring your existing setup without any issues.
