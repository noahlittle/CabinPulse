Frequently Asked Questions About CabinPulse's Temperature Monitor

How many temperature thresholds can I set?

You can set unlimited temperature thresholds with CabinPulse, both for high and low temperatures. This allows you to create a comprehensive monitoring system with different alerts for various conditions - from freezing alerts to overheat warnings and everything in between.

Does CabinPulse need WiFi to monitor temperature?

No, WiFi is NOT required. Unlike most temperature monitors that depend on an internet connection, CabinPulse uses cellular technology to transmit temperature data and alerts. This makes it ideal for remote locations, vacation properties, or any setting where internet connectivity is unreliable.

How will I receive temperature alerts?

CabinPulse sends instant alerts via both text message and email when temperatures cross any of your customized thresholds. You can add multiple contacts to receive alerts, and even configure different contacts for different temperature thresholds.

How accurate is the temperature monitoring?

CabinPulse provides exceptional accuracy of ±0.2°C (±0.36°F), which is significantly more precise than standard temperature monitors. This high accuracy is essential for applications requiring precise temperature control like wine cellars, sensitive equipment rooms, or greenhouse monitoring.

What happens during a power outage?

CabinPulse features a built-in backup battery that keeps the device functioning for up to 3 days during power outages. You'll receive an immediate alert when power is lost, and the device will continue monitoring temperature and sending alerts throughout the outage period.

Does CabinPulse record temperature history?

Yes, CabinPulse maintains a complete historical record of temperature readings. This data helps you identify patterns, troubleshoot environmental control issues, and optimize your property's climate management systems. You can view temperature trends by hour, day, week, or month through the dashboard.

Can I adjust temperature thresholds remotely?

Yes, you can set and adjust all temperature thresholds remotely through the CabinPulse dashboard from anywhere with internet access. This allows you to modify your monitoring parameters as needs change without having to be physically present at the device location.

What are common applications for temperature monitoring?

CabinPulse is ideal for monitoring temperatures in vacation homes, cabins, wine cellars, art galleries, equipment rooms, server rooms, greenhouses, pet environments, food storage facilities, and anywhere precise temperature monitoring is essential. The ability to set multiple custom thresholds makes it versatile for many applications.
