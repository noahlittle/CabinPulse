Frequently Asked Questions

What temperature do pipes freeze at?

Pipes can freeze as soon as 0°C (32°F) depending on insulation and other factors. CabinPulse lets you set alerts for 3°C, 5°C or whatever threshold you'd like so you have plenty of warning before they reach freezing point.

Does it need WiFi?

No. CabinPulse uses cellular technology (LTE with 3G fallback) to transmit data. It does not rely on your home internet router, making it immune to internet outages.

How do I get alerts?

You receive instant SMS text messages and Emails. You can add unlimited contacts to the notification list.

Does it work in extreme cold?

CabinPulse is designed to be used indoors, but the device can operate and accurately measure temperatures from -20°C to 60°C (-4°F to 140°F) while plugged in.

Where should I place it?

Place it in the area most vulnerable to cold, such as a basement, utility room, or near plumbing on an exterior wall.

Can I monitor multiple areas?

Yes, you can monitor multiple areas by using CabinPulse Temperature Add-Ons. Each Add-On can be placed in a different location to provide comprehensive coverage, without increasing your subscription cost.

Does CabinPulse support a leak detection feature?

Yes, CabinPulse can support leak detection by using our Leak Sensor Add-On. These Add-Ons can be placed in areas prone to leaks, providing real-time alerts and monitoring.
