Why Campground WiFi Isn't Enough

Relying on campground WiFi or standard thermometers puts your pets at risk. Power failures and spotty internet are common in RV parks.

WiFi is Unreliable

Campground WiFi is often overloaded or weak. If the internet drops, your WiFi thermometer stops sending alerts, leaving your pets vulnerable.

Power Failures

If shore power disconnects, your AC stops. An RV can heat up to dangerous levels in minutes. CabinPulse works even during outages.

Heat Stroke Risks

Pets are highly sensitive to heat. Immediate notification of temperature spikes is critical to preventing tragedy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does it need WiFi to protect my pets?

No! CabinPulse uses cellular technology, making it perfect for RVs in campgrounds or boondocking where WiFi is unreliable. Your pet's safety alerts will always get through.

What if my RV loses shore power?

CabinPulse has a built-in backup battery lasting up to 3 days. It will alert you of the power loss and continue monitoring the temperature inside the RV.

How many alerts can I set?

You can set unlimited temperature thresholds. Define specific "too hot" and "too cold" points for your dog, cat, or other pets to ensure they are safe.

Can I alert my partner or pet sitter?

Yes, you can add multiple contacts to receive SMS and Email alerts, ensuring someone is always notified of dangerous conditions.
