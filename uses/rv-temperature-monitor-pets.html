RV Pet Temperature Monitor: Your Questions Answered

How many temperature thresholds can I set?

You can set unlimited temperature thresholds with CabinPulse for your RV. This means you can define specific "too hot" and "too cold" points for your dog, cat, or other pets, ensuring they're always in a safe environment.

Does CabinPulse need WiFi to protect my pets in the RV?

No, WiFi is NOT required! CabinPulse uses reliable cellular technology, making it perfect for RVs in campgrounds, boondocking spots, or anywhere WiFi is spotty or unavailable. Your pet's safety alerts will always get through.

How will I get alerts if my RV gets too hot or cold for my pet?

CabinPulse sends instant alerts via both text message and email when the RV temperature goes outside your pet's custom safe zone. You can add multiple contacts (e.g., spouse, pet sitter) to ensure someone is always notified.

How accurate is it for pet safety?

CabinPulse offers exceptional accuracy of ±0.2°C (±0.36°F). This precision is vital for pets, especially those sensitive to small temperature changes, ensuring their environment is truly safe and comfortable.

What if my RV loses power (shore or battery)?

CabinPulse has a built-in backup battery lasting up to 3 days. You'll get an alert if RV power is lost, and it will continue monitoring the temperature and sending pet safety alerts, which is crucial if your AC/heater depends on that power.

Is it safe to leave my pet alone in the RV with CabinPulse?

CabinPulse is a powerful monitoring tool that alerts you to dangerous temperature changes. However, it does not control your RV's climate. Always use caution, know your pet's limits, and be aware of local conditions and regulations when leaving pets unattended.

Where should I place it in my RV for best pet monitoring?

Place CabinPulse near where your pet spends most of their time, but away from direct sunlight, vents, or heat-generating appliances for the most accurate ambient RV temperature reading. This ensures alerts are based on your pet's actual environment.

Can I use it for different types of pets in my RV?

Absolutely! CabinPulse is ideal for monitoring the safety of dogs, cats, birds, reptiles (that need specific heat), and any pet sensitive to temperature. The custom alerts let you tailor it to any pet's unique needs in your camper or motorhome.
