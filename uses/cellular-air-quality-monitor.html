Air Quality Monitoring: Your Questions Answered

What air pollutants does CabinPulse detect?

CabinPulse primarily monitors for a broad range of Volatile Organic Compounds (VOCs), which are common indoor air pollutants from sources like building materials, cleaning products, and smoke. It also calculates an overall Indoor Air Quality (IAQ) score based on multiple factors.

Can it detect wildfire smoke?

Yes, while not a specific smoke particle detector, CabinPulse is highly effective at detecting the VOCs and other pollutants characteristic of wildfire smoke. Significant increases in these will trigger alerts, indicating poor air quality likely due to smoke.

What is an IAQ Score?

The Indoor Air Quality (IAQ) score is an index that provides a simple way to understand the overall quality of your air. It ranges from 0 (good) to 500 (hazardous). CabinPulse allows you to set alerts based on different IAQ score levels.

Does CabinPulse need WiFi for air monitoring?

Absolutely not. CabinPulse uses its own cellular connection to transmit air quality data and send alerts. This is vital for remote locations without internet or during power outages when WiFi routers are down.

How are air quality alerts delivered?

You'll receive instant alerts via text message (SMS) and email when your pre-set IAQ or VOC thresholds are breached. You can add multiple contacts to ensure the right people are notified.

What if the power goes out?

CabinPulse has a built-in battery backup lasting up to 3 days. It continues to monitor air quality and send alerts during power outages, which is often when indoor air can deteriorate due to lack of ventilation.

Can I see historical air quality data?

Yes, the CabinPulse dashboard provides access to historical trends for IAQ scores. This helps you identify patterns, sources of pollution, or the impact of events like wildfires or renovations.

Where is CabinPulse best used for air monitoring?

It's ideal for vacation homes, cabins in wildfire-prone areas, RVs, basements, workshops, properties undergoing renovation, or any indoor space where you want to ensure healthy air without relying on WiFi.
