Essential Vacation Home Monitoring Features

Power Outage Resilient Alerts

Get immediate notifications when power fails at your vacation home. The built-in battery backup ensures you're alerted even during extended outages, protecting against frozen pipes and spoiled food.

Temperature Extremes Protection

Set custom alerts for both freeze protection and overheating. Essential for preventing pipe damage in winter and ensuring guest comfort in vacation rentals.

Air Quality & Wildfire Monitoring

Monitor Indoor Air Quality (IAQ) and get alerts during wildfire season. Know if your vacation home is safe to visit or if air purification is needed before guests arrive.

Humidity & Mold Prevention

Track humidity levels to prevent costly mold and structural damage. Especially important for vacation homes that sit empty for extended periods.

Smoke Alarm Sound Detection

CabinPulse listens for smoke alarm sound patterns (T3) and sends informational alerts. An extra layer of awareness for your vacation property's safety. Informational only - not a life safety device.

Multi-Carrier Cellular Connectivity

Works on ALL major networks in North America without WiFi. If there's any cellular signal at your vacation home, CabinPulse will connect and monitor 24/7.

Vacation Home Monitoring Questions

How does CabinPulse work during power outages?

CabinPulse has a built-in battery that provides up to 3 days of backup power. It immediately alerts you when power goes out and continues monitoring temperature and other conditions until power is restored.

Can I really cancel my vacation home's WiFi service?

Yes! CabinPulse uses cellular connectivity and doesn't need WiFi. Many customers cancel expensive services like Starlink ($140+/month) and save over $1000 annually while maintaining essential monitoring.

Is CabinPulse really portable between properties?

Absolutely! Simply unplug from one location and plug into another. CabinPulse automatically connects to the best available cellular network at each location. Perfect for managing multiple vacation rentals or seasonal properties.

Is this suitable for vacation rental properties?

Perfect for vacation rentals! Monitor conditions between guests, get alerts for temperature issues that could affect guest experience, and detect air quality problems before check-ins. Many property managers use CabinPulse across their portfolio.

Will CabinPulse work at my remote vacation home?

If your vacation home has any cellular signal from any carrier, CabinPulse will work. It connects to all major North American networks and automatically chooses the strongest signal available.

How does wildfire monitoring help vacation homes?

CabinPulse monitors air quality and can detect wildfire smoke infiltration. Get alerts before visiting your vacation home during fire season, or know when to run air purifiers to maintain healthy conditions for guests.

What about freeze protection for vacation homes?

Set custom low-temperature alerts to prevent frozen pipes and costly damage. Even if power fails, the battery backup ensures you're notified of dangerous temperature drops so you can take action.

What's the total cost compared to keeping WiFi year-round?

CabinPulse costs $199 for the device plus $14 CAD/month ($168/year) or $10 CAD/month ($120/year) with a 3-year commitment. Compare this to year-round Starlink at $140+/month ($1680+/year). You'll save over $1000 annually while getting better monitoring.
