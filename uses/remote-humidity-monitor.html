Frequently Asked Questions About CabinPulse's Humidity Monitoring

What are ideal/dangerous humidity levels?

Ideal indoor humidity is typically 30-50%. Levels consistently above 60% can promote mold and mildew growth, while very low levels can damage wood or cause discomfort. CabinPulse lets you set custom alerts for both high and low humidity.

Does CabinPulse need WiFi for humidity monitoring?

No, WiFi is NOT required. CabinPulse uses reliable cellular technology (LTE with 3G fallback) to transmit humidity data and alerts, perfect for remote properties or areas with unstable internet.

How will I receive humidity alerts?

CabinPulse sends instant alerts via text message and email when humidity levels go outside your customized thresholds. You can add multiple contacts to ensure someone is always notified.

Can CabinPulse detect both high and low humidity?

Yes, you can set custom upper (high humidity) and lower (low humidity) thresholds in the CabinPulse dashboard. You'll be alerted if humidity goes above or below these set points.

What happens during a power outage?

CabinPulse has a backup battery, allowing it to continue monitoring humidity and send alerts for up to 3 days during a power outage. You'll also be alerted instantly when power is lost and restored.

Where should I place CabinPulse for humidity monitoring?

Place CabinPulse in areas prone to moisture like basements, crawl spaces, bathrooms, attics, or near plumbing. It's also ideal for storage areas with sensitive items like electronics, documents, or musical instruments.

Can I adjust humidity alert thresholds?

Yes, you can easily set and adjust your high and low humidity alert thresholds at any time through the online CabinPulse dashboard from anywhere in the world. You can set as many different thresholds as you'd like.

How often is humidity data recorded?

CabinPulse records humidity data every 5 minutes, providing a detailed historical log accessible via your dashboard. You can also request an on-demand, real-time humidity reading at any time.
