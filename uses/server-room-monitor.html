Your Server Room Monitoring Questions Answered

What critical conditions does CabinPulse monitor in a server room?

CabinPulse monitors for power outages, ambient temperature, air quality, relative humidity, and listens for standard T3 smoke alarm sound patterns.

How does it help with power outages affecting servers?

CabinPulse sends immediate text/email alerts if power to the room (and thus potentially your UPS input) fails and when it's restored. Its built-in battery keeps the monitor running, ensuring you're notified even if servers are on UPS but mains power is out.

Can it prevent server overheating and HVAC failures?

By providing timely high-temperature alerts, CabinPulse gives early warning of HVAC malfunctions or insufficient cooling, allowing you to intervene before servers overheat, shut down, or suffer permanent damage.

How is air quality monitoring useful in a server room?

CabinPulse monitors VOCs, which can indicate issues like overheating components or other environmental factors that might degrade server performance or lifespan. Early detection can prompt maintenance.

Why is humidity monitoring important for IT equipment?

Incorrect humidity levels can be detrimental. High humidity risks condensation and corrosion on circuit boards. Low humidity can increase static electricity (ESD), endangering sensitive components. CabinPulse alerts you to out-of-range humidity.

What about smoke alarm sound detection in server rooms?

CabinPulse's acoustic sensor can detect T3 smoke alarm patterns. If your server room's smoke detector activates, CabinPulse sends an informational alert, offering an additional layer of awareness for potential fire, especially in unmanned locations.

Does it need my server room's LAN or WiFi?

No. CabinPulse operates independently using its built-in cellular modem. It connects to all major networks across Canada and the US. This means it continues to send alerts even if your primary internet connection or local network is down.

Is it a cost-effective solution for server rooms?

Yes. The cost of CabinPulse is minimal compared to the potential losses from server downtime, data corruption, hardware replacement due to environmental issues, or emergency call-out fees. It's proactive protection for valuable IT assets.
