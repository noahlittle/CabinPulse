CabinPulse Add-Ons

Expand your monitoring with wireless sensors that connect to your CabinPulse device. No WiFi or power needed at sensor locations – monitor anywhere within 30 meters of your base unit.

Choose Your Add-Ons

Each Add-On connects wirelessly to your CabinPulse device and is battery powered. No additional power or WiFi needed.

Temperature Sensor

Monitor temperature in multiple areas with precision. Perfect for freezers, server rooms, greenhouses, or any space requiring specific temperature control. Set custom high and low thresholds for instant alerts.

  • Customizable high/low alert thresholds
  • ±0.2°C precision accuracy
  • 2-year battery life
  • Works from -40°C to 60°C
$59 CAD

Motion Sensor

Advanced PIR motion detection for reliable property monitoring. Know exactly when someone enters your cabin, storage area, or restricted zones. Perfect for security and occupancy monitoring.

  • 5m detection range
  • 110° field of view
  • Instant motion alerts
  • 2-year battery life
$59 CAD

Door/Window Sensor

Monitor all entry points to your property. Magnetic reed switch technology provides reliable detection of door and window openings. Essential for security and access monitoring.

  • Magnetic reed switch
  • Works on doors and windows
  • Instant open/close alerts
  • 2-year battery life
  • Easy peel-and-stick installation
$59 CAD

Leak Sensor with Cable

Advanced water detection with included 1-meter leak cable and pogo pin connectors. The cable allows monitoring along entire lengths - perfect for water heaters, sump pumps, and pipe runs.

  • Detects as little as 1mm of water
  • Use with included leak cable or with built-in pogo contacts
  • Includes 1m leak detection cable
  • 2-year battery life
  • Adjustable sensitivity levels
$59 CAD Includes 1m cable

Smart Switch Relay

Remote control relay for turning devices on/off from anywhere. Control pumps, heaters, lights, or any device up to 3A. Features screw terminal blocks for easy wiring and can be triggered manually or automatically based on sensor conditions.

  • Remote on/off control
  • Screw terminal block connections
  • 2-year battery life
$59 CAD Relay rated for 3A
