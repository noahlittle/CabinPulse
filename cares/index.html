About CabinPulse Cares

Supporting Communities, One Device at a Time

CabinPulse Cares is our commitment to giving back to the communities that make Canada strong. We understand that many non-profit organizations, community centres, and charitable institutions operate on limited budgets while providing essential services.

Through this program, qualifying organizations receive a FREE CabinPulse device and three years of monitoring service â€“ a combined value of over $500. This helps protect valuable community assets from temperature extremes, power outages, and other environmental threats.

With CabinPulse Cares, we're protecting churches, fire halls, community centres, food banks, and many other vital community spaces from costly damage and disruption.

Who Qualifies for CabinPulse Cares?

Places of Worship

Churches, temples, mosques, synagogues, and other religious institutions serving their communities.

Emergency Services

Volunteer fire departments, search and rescue organizations, and community emergency response teams.

Non-Profit Organizations

Registered Canadian charitable organizations providing community services.

Community Centres

Recreation centres, senior centres, youth organizations, and community gathering spaces.

Food Security

Food banks, community kitchens, meal programs, and organizations fighting food insecurity.

Social Services

Shelters, transitional housing, support centres, and organizations helping vulnerable populations.

Terms & Conditions
  • Recipients receive one (1) CabinPulse device and thirty-six (36) months of monitoring service at no cost.
  • Organizations must be registered non-profits, charities, or community service organizations.
  • CabinPulse reserves the right to publicize the partnership, including use of organization name and logo.
  • Recipients agree to provide testimonials and facility photos for promotional use.
  • After the third year, organizations may opt to continue service at standard discounted non-profit rate.
  • Applications are reviewed monthly, with recipients notified within 30 days.
  • CabinPulse reserves the right to modify or discontinue the program at any time.
  • One device per organization, per location. Multiple locations may apply separately.
