About CabinPulse

CabinPulse is a revolutionary cellular-based monitoring system designed specifically for cabins and vacation properties. Using the cellular network for connectivity, CabinPulse monitors your cabin's temperature, humidity, power status, and air quality - all without requiring WiFi.

How It Works

  • Uses LTE with 3G fallback to transmit data from your cabin
  • Simply plug in the device - no complex installation or setup required
  • Monitor your cabin from anywhere using our mobile app or website
  • Receive instant alerts for temperature drops, power outages, or other issues

Coverage in Saskatchewan

CabinPulse operates on the robust LTE network with 3G fallback, which provides broad coverage across Saskatchewan, including many remote lake areas. This network ensures reliable connectivity for your cabin monitoring needs.

Note: While coverage is generally widespread, if your lake shows partial coverage ("Works at SOME cabins"), we strongly recommend checking the detailed coverage map linked above for your specific cabin location. Coverage can vary based on local terrain and tower proximity.
