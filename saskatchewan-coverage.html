About CabinPulse

CabinPulse is a revolutionary cellular-based monitoring system designed specifically for cabins and vacation properties. Using advanced LTE-M technology, CabinPulse monitors your cabin's temperature, humidity, power status, and air quality - all without requiring WiFi or internet connection.

How It Works

  • Uses the LTE-M cellular network to transmit data from your cabin
  • Simply plug in the device - no complex installation or setup required
  • Monitor your cabin from anywhere using our mobile app or website
  • Receive alerts for temperature drops, power outages, or other issues

Coverage in Saskatchewan

CabinPulse operates on the LTE-M network, which provides coverage to many areas across Saskatchewan. Unfortunately, coverage is more limited in Saskatchewan compared to many other parts of Canada and the United States. Many cell towers in Saskatchewan have not been upgraded to support LTE-M, meaning that while there may be cell service, devices like CabinPulse that rely on the LTE-M network may not work everywhere. Coverage can vary depending on your specific location, especially in remote areas.

Note: If your lake shows partial coverage, we strongly recommend checking the detailed coverage map linked above for your specific location. Coverage can vary significantly even within the same lake area.
