Frequently Asked Questions

Does CabinPulse need WiFi to work?

No, WiFi is NOT required. CabinPulse uses cellular technology to transmit data, eliminating the need for WiFi. The device automatically connects to available cellular networks in your area.

Do I need to pay for another cellular plan?

No! The CabinPulse subscription covers everything. The cellular connectivity is completely built-in to the device, and there are no cellular data plans or WiFi required.

How do I setup my CabinPulse device?

Once you receive your device, login to your CabinPulse dashboard, enter the activation code on your device, and plug it into a power outlet. It will immediately begin monitoring!

My cabin doesn't have cell coverage - will CabinPulse work?

CabinPulse uses cellular technology to transmit data so coverage is required.


If you're unsure of coverage in your area, check the coverage map of our network partners in Canada or the US.

What's included in the device fee?

The one-time $99 device fee covers your CabinPulse monitoring unit, which includes temperature, humidity, air quality and power outage detection capabilities.

Can I buy multiple devices?

If you'd like to monitor multiple cabins or spaces, you can purchase additional devices for each location and manage them from the same account. Do note that each device requires its own subscription.

How up-to-date is the CabinPulse data?

CabinPulse collects data every 5 minutes, and sends new data updates to your dashboard every 15 minutes.

How are alerts sent?

You'll receive alerts via text and email for any critical issues detected by your CabinPulse device. You can customize the alert thresholds and choose which alerts you'd like to receive.

What's the battery life of the CabinPulse device?

The CabinPulse device is designed to be plugged into a power outlet. However, it does have a backup battery that lasts up to 7 days in case of prolonged power outages.

How much electricity does CabinPulse use if it's plugged in 24/7?

The CabinPulse device is extremely energy efficient and uses about as much electricity as running your microwave for 60s - in an entire month!.

How far back can I view historical data?

We store data captured from your device for as long as your subscription is active. If you choose to not renew your subscription, your data will be stored for 30 days before being deleted.

Can the device be used outdoors or in unheated buildings?

CabinPulse is intended to be used indoors only, but can be used in unheated buildings. Note that it is rated to continue functioning from -20°C to 60°C (-4°F to 140°F) while plugged into power; its onboard battery life is significantly diminished in cold temperatures.

What's the warranty on the CabinPulse device?

The CabinPulse device comes with a 1-year warranty. If you experience any issues, contact our support team for troubleshooting or a replacement.

Where is the CabinPulse device manufactured?

We're proudly based in Saskatchewan, Canada; all of our devices are designed and manufactured in Saskatoon.

Where are your servers located?

All data is securely hosted on servers located in Canada.

More Questions?

We're always happy to help! Contact our support team at support@cabinpulse.com with any questions, thoughts or concerns and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
