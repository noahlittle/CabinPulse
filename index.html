Frequently Asked Questions

How does CabinPulse work without WiFi?

CabinPulse uses cellular technology to transmit data, eliminating the need for WiFi. The device automatically connects to available cellular networks in your area.

Do I need to pay for another cellular plan?

No! The monthly CabinPulse fee covers everything. The cellular connectivity is completely built-in to the device, and there are no cellular data plans or WiFi required.

How do I setup my CabinPulse device?

Once you receive your device, login to your CabinPulse dashboard, enter the activation code on your device, and plug it into a power outlet. It will immediately begin monitoring!

My cabin doesn't have cell coverage - will CabinPulse work?

CabinPulse uses cellular technology to transmit data so coverage is required.


If you're unsure of coverage in your area, check the coverage map of our network partner.

What's included in the device fee?

The one-time $99 device fee covers your CabinPulse monitoring unit, which includes temperature, humidity, air quality and power outage detection capabilities.

How up-to-date is the CabinPulse data?

CabinPulse collects data every 5 minutes, and sends new data updates to your dashboard every 15 minutes. In case of any critical events (like a sudden temperature drop), alerts are sent immediately.

Is there a limit to the number of alerts I can receive?

No, there's no limit to the number of alerts. You'll receive notifications for all critical events to ensure the safety of your cabin.

Can I customize the alert thresholds?

Yes, you can customize alert thresholds through your secure online dashboard. This allows you to tailor the system to your cabin's specific needs.

What's the battery life of the CabinPulse device?

The CabinPulse device is designed to be plugged into a power outlet. However, it does have a backup battery that lasts up to 24 hours in case of prolonged power outages.
