Frequently Asked Questions

How does CabinPulse work without WiFi?

CabinPulse uses cellular technology to transmit data, eliminating the need for WiFi. The device automatically connects to available networks in your area.

What's included in the device fee?

The one-time $60 device fee covers your CabinPulse monitoring unit, which includes temperature, humidity, smoke, and gas sensors, as well as power outage detection capabilities.

How often does CabinPulse send updates?

CabinPulse sends regular updates every 15 minutes. In case of any critical events (like a sudden temperature drop or smoke detection), alerts are sent immediately.

Is there a limit to the number of alerts I can receive?

No, there's no limit to the number of alerts. You'll receive notifications for all critical events to ensure the safety of your cabin.

Can I customize the alert thresholds?

Yes, you can customize alert thresholds through your secure online dashboard. This allows you to tailor the system to your cabin's specific needs.

What's the battery life of the CabinPulse device?

The CabinPulse device is designed to be plugged into a power outlet. However, it does have a backup battery that lasts up to 24 hours in case of prolonged power outages.
